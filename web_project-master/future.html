The Future of Artificial intelligence

  • * Job displacement: AI systems may automate certain jobs, potentially causing job loss in certain industries.
  • * Improved efficiency and productivity: AI can aoutomate routine tasks and improve decision-making, leading to increased efficiency and productivity in many industries.
